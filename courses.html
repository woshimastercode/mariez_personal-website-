Courses

The courses I have taken throughout my academic experience have played an important role in shaping both my analytical thinking and creative perspective. Many of these classes required not only understanding theoretical concepts but also applying them through projects, discussions, and practical exercises. Through these experiences, I developed the ability to approach complex problems step by step, examine patterns carefully, and communicate ideas clearly. Over time, I became more comfortable working with challenging material and exploring new approaches to learning.

Courses in statistics, data science, and programming helped me build a strong technical foundation. These classes introduced concepts such as regression analysis, machine learning models, data visualization, and computational problem solving. Through hands-on assignments and group projects, I learned how to clean and analyze datasets, build predictive models, and interpret the results in meaningful ways. These experiences also helped me understand how data-driven methods can be applied to real-world questions in areas such as finance, healthcare, and social research.

In addition to technical courses, I also explored classes related to art education and creative learning. These courses emphasized reflection, observation, and visual communication. Through activities such as visual storytelling, creative teaching exercises, and classroom discussions, I learned how artistic practices can encourage critical thinking and self-expression. These experiences helped me see how creativity and analytical thinking can complement each other and contribute to deeper learning.

Combining analytical coursework with creative education has been an important part of my academic journey. Technical courses strengthened my problem-solving abilities and computational thinking, while art-related courses helped me develop creativity, empathy, and new ways of communicating ideas. Together, these courses helped shape the interdisciplinary perspective that continues to influence my projects and interests today.
